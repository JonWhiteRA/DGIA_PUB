
I. Dissemination of Information





The Superintendent/Designee will establish a process to communicate expectations for civil behavior outlined in the policy to all school administrators and central office/supervisory personnel.



The principal/supervisor designee will establish a process to communicate expectations of civil behavior annually to their employees, all students, parents, service providers, and community groups.




II. Addressing Concerns and Issues





Resolution through Mutual Agreement


Individuals who feel they have been subjected to uncivil conduct are first encouraged to respectfully resolve the concern/issue with the person or persons directly involved, in a manner that reflects a restorative approach.

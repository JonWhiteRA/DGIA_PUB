
I. Policy Statement



The Board of Education of Howard County recognizes that many contests sponsored by local, state, and national outside organizations are both meritorious and well intended. The Board further encourages student participation in contests provided they relate to the educational program of studies and benefit students educationally.


II. Purpose



The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for student participation in contests sponsored by outside organizations.

IV. Standards





The contest must benefit students educationally, relate to the educational program, and be weighed against loss of instructional time.



Student participation in contests is limited to those that:




Provide for appropriate and adequate supervision for the duration of the event.



Incur participation cost that is minimal to the students, school, and community.



Do not promote or advertise a product or an ideology.



Are consistent with HCPSS goals.




V. Responsibilities





Administrative Directors will review the provisions of this policy with school-based administrators annually.



Administrative Directors will approve participation in contests.



School administrators will approve the participation of their schools in contests.



The Office of School Administration will create and maintain a database of approved contests.




VI. Delegation of Authority



The Superintendent is authorized to develop procedures for the implementation of this policy.

Article 94

Paragraph 1.0:
• Directive 95/46/ECis repealed with effect from 25 May 2018.

Paragraph 2.0:
• 1References to the repealed Directive shall be construed as references to this Regulation.2References to the Working Party on the Protection of Individuals with regard to the Processing of Personal Data established by Article 29 ofDirective 95/46/ECshall be construed as references to the European Data Protection Board established by this Regulation.

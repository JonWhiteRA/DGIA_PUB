Article 93

Paragraph 1.0:
• 1The Commission shall be assisted by a committee.2That committee shall be a committee within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011.

Paragraph 2.0:
• Where reference is made to this paragraph, Article 5 of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011shall apply.

Paragraph 3.0:
• Where reference is made to this paragraph, Article 8 of Regulation ((EU) No 182/2011, in conjunction with Article 5 thereof, shall apply.

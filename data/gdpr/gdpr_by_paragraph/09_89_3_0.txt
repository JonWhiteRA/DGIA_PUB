Where personal data are processed for archiving purposes in the public interest, Union or Member State law may provide for derogations from the rights referred to inArticles 15,16,18,19,20and21subject to the conditions and safeguards referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article in so far as such rights are likely to render impossible or seriously impair the achievement of the specific purposes, and such derogations are necessary for the fulfilment of those purposes.

Where a draft code of conduct relates to processing activities in several Member States, the supervisory authority which is competent pursuant toArticle 55shall, before approving the draft code, amendment or extension, submit it in the procedure referred to inArticle 63to the Board which shall provide an opinion on whether the draft code, amendment or extension complies with this Regulation or, in the situation referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article, provides appropriate safeguards.
